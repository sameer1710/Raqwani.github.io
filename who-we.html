THE COMPANY

It is our great pleasure to introduce Yahya Ahmed Raqwani Co.Ltd (Raqwani Company) as General and Specialized construction Contractors and 100% Saudi owned company Located in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia

Raqwani Company was formed in the year 2007 to facilitate in the development of Saudi Arabia’s mainly in the field Cranes and Heavy Equipment renting resources as company which has all services under one brand name in Saudi Arabia.

The company’s key strength are its varied experience, rich knowledge of local conditions, high standards of health, safety, quality & environment, its ability to manage operations in diverse industries, long term relationships with world class clients and ability to mobilize financial resources.

We owe our steady growth and success to our faithfulness to our company philosophy of sustained personal and professional integrity; to our steadfast commitment to excellence and client satisfaction; and to the dedication and quality of our people, the most valuable of our assets.

Our reliability and consistency have rewarded us with repeat and long-term business relations, associations, and partnerships with clients, developers, general contractors, subcontractors, government agencies, and peer companies on local, regional, and international levels.

While adhering fully to our original founding values of quality, trust, diversity, and integration, we keep evolving and unfailingly rise to the challenges of change. And while we conduct our business to the highest and latest international standards and best practices, we are particularly mindful of the environment and its protection and are especially proud of our long-standing record of safety that is unrivaled in the Kingdom and in many parts of the region.

Yahya Ahmed Raqwani Co. Ltd is ISO 9001 certified Organization. Quality Company built by quality people. Yahya Ahmed Raqwani Co. Ltd prizes people above all other resources. Our people have always been the source of our strength. In addition to training and professional development, we’re particularly proud of our long-standing record of safety, as we always empower our workforce to seize the responsibility for customer satisfaction, and grow personally and professionally.

The Raqwani Company
