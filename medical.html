RAQWANI MEDICAL

Raqwani is one of the leading Medical suppliers in Kingdome addressing most of major medical requirements and challenges of OIL RIG companies and Hygienic Products for Food Industries

We provide our clients the latest advances in technology, equipment, leadership in solutions, diversified services for healthcare and scientific technology sectors.

Our products have competitive price with high quality and has enjoyed a high reputation in the industry. The quality assurance of our products covers those aspects of manufacturer concerned with securing & maintaining sterile conditions of all our devices & equipments as of FDA approved & conforms to the requirements of the clients.

Varied experience & rich knowledge, high standards of health, safety, quality & environment. Our ability to manage operations on a long term relationship with our happy customers and ability to mobilize financial resources.

We are one stop shop for your entire Medical Requirements. We supplies all Medical & Healthcare Products, Medical Equipments, Medical emergency products, Paramedical & medical products for OIL RIG companies and Hygienic Products for Food Industries. We are also specialized in Ambulance services. We look forward to serving you in the near future.

We are specialized in Medical Consumable items, Surgical Dressing Products, Medical Clinical Equipments, Respiratory Products, Medical Laboratory Supplies, Rehabilitation Products, Paramedical Products, Hygienic products, Healthcare Products, Housekeeping Supplies and Hospital Equipments.
