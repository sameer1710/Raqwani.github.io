CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

We always operate with the highest levels of integrity and responsibility so as to build on the trust that we have earned over the years.

Yahya Ahmed Raqwani
(Chairman)

Yahya Ahmed Raqwani Co.Ltd. (Raqwani Company) has grown nearly fourfold in the last five years and has developed new capabilities including ‘contracting’. We take pride in counting the outputs as related to manufacturing, services and other industries as our esteemed clients. We always operate with the highest levels of integrity and responsibility so as to build on the trust that we have earned over the years.

Our employees are our biggest asset. Leveraging their collective talent allows us to undertake complex projects even in the harshest of operating environments. As a company that operates across the length and breadth of GCC and overseas, we believe our responsibilities go beyond our clients, to the larger community that we operate in.
