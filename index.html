Know Our Vission

Contractors and 100% Saudi owned company Located in Al Khobar,
Saudi Arabia. Raqwani Company was formed in the year 2007 to
facilitate in the development of Saudi Arabia's mainly in the field Cranes
and Heavy Equipment renting resources as company which has all
services under one brand name in Saudi Arabia.

The company's key strength are its varied experience, rich knowledge of
local conditions, high standards of health, safety, quality & environment,
its ability to manage operations in diverse industries, long term
relationships with world class clients and ability to mobilize financial
resources.
